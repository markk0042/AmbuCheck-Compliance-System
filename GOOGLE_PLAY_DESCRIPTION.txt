AmbuCheck - Ambulance Equipment Compliance Checklist

AmbuCheck is a comprehensive mobile application designed for ambulance service personnel to ensure vehicle and medical equipment compliance, safety, and readiness.

KEY FEATURES:

✅ Vehicle Defect Inspection (VDI)
Complete start-of-shift and end-of-shift vehicle checks including lighting, fluids, medical gases, cockpit, patient area, and required documentation.

✅ Equipment & Bag Checks
Comprehensive checklists for:
• BLS (Basic Life Support) bags
• EMT medication pouches
• ALS (Advanced Life Support) bags
• Paramedic and Advanced Paramedic medication bags
• Monitor/AED equipment verification

✅ Medication Management
Track medication expiry dates, quantities, and tamper seal verification for all medication bags and pouches.

✅ Incident Reporting
Vehicle IR1 incident report form with photo attachment capability for documenting accidents, damage, or safety events.

✅ Photo Documentation
Capture and attach photos for vehicle inspections, equipment verification, and incident reporting.

✅ Secure Authentication
Role-based access with PIN protection for crew members, ensuring only authorized personnel can complete checks.

✅ Offline Capability
Complete forms and checklists even when connectivity is limited, with automatic synchronization when connection is restored.

BENEFITS:

• Ensures compliance with medical equipment and vehicle safety standards
• Reduces paperwork and administrative burden
• Provides digital audit trail for all inspections and checks
• Helps prevent equipment shortages and expired medications
• Streamlines shift start and end procedures
• Improves patient safety through systematic equipment verification

WHO IT'S FOR:

AmbuCheck is designed for:
• Ambulance service personnel
• EMTs and Paramedics
• Advanced Paramedics
• Fleet managers
• Compliance officers

REQUIREMENTS:

This app requires an active account with your ambulance service organization. Contact your administrator for access credentials.
