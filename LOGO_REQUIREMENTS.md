# AmbuCheck Logo Requirements for Google Play Store

## App Name
**AmbuCheck**

## App Purpose
Ambulance equipment compliance and vehicle inspection checklist application for medical professionals.

## Logo Specifications

### App Icon (Required)
- **Size**: 512 x 512 pixels
- **Format**: PNG (32-bit with alpha channel)
- **Shape**: Square (Google will apply rounded corners automatically)
- **Background**: Transparent or solid color
- **Style**: Modern, professional, medical/healthcare theme
- **Colors**: Blue and white (suggested: #3498db or #2c3e50 for blue, white for contrast)
- **Text**: Minimal or no text (icon should be recognizable without text)
- **Theme**: Ambulance, medical equipment, checklist, compliance

### Design Elements to Consider
- Medical cross symbol
- Ambulance vehicle silhouette
- Checklist/checkmark icon
- Medical equipment (stethoscope, bag, etc.)
- Professional, clean, modern design
- Suitable for small sizes (icon will be displayed at various sizes)

### Feature Graphic (Required)
- **Size**: 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format**: JPG or PNG (24-bit)
- **Purpose**: Banner at top of Google Play Store listing
- **Content**: App name "AmbuCheck" with tagline/subtitle
- **Style**: Should match app icon design

## Design Brief for Designer

**App Name**: AmbuCheck  
**Tagline**: "Ambulance Equipment Compliance Checklist"

**Target Audience**: Medical professionals, ambulance service personnel, EMTs, Paramedics

**Brand Identity**:
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Medical/Healthcare focused
- Compliance and safety oriented
- Modern and clean

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Blue (#3498db or similar medical blue)
- Secondary: White
- Accent: Could include red for medical cross or green for checkmarks

**Style Guidelines**:
- Clean, minimalist design
- Professional medical aesthetic
- Should work well at small sizes (app icon)
- Should convey: Safety, Compliance, Medical Equipment, Checklists

## Deliverables Needed

1. **App Icon**: 512x512px PNG (transparent background preferred)
2. **Feature Graphic**: 1024x500px JPG/PNG
3. **Optional**: Different sizes for future use (256x256, 128x128, etc.)

## Where to Use

- App launcher icon (for Android devices)

- Website (if applicable)
