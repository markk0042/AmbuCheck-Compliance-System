# Privacy Policy for AmbuCheck

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## Introduction

AmbuCheck ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AmbuCheck mobile application and web services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **User Account Information**: Username, PIN number, and practitioner name
- **Form Submissions**: Data entered into equipment checklists, medication forms, vehicle defect reports, and incident reports
- **Location Data**: Vehicle call signs and location information entered by users
- **Photos**: Images uploaded as part of equipment checks and incident reports

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns and form completion data
- **Authentication Tokens**: Secure tokens for maintaining your login session

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the AmbuCheck service
- Authenticate users and manage access to the application
- Process and store equipment checklists, medication forms, and incident reports
- Ensure compliance with medical equipment and vehicle safety standards
- Generate reports for operational and compliance purposes
- Improve our services and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Data Storage**: All data is stored securely on our servers hosted by Render.com
- **Security Measures**: We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data
- **Access Control**: Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our services (e.g., hosting providers)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to comply with legal processes
- **Safety and Security**: To protect the rights, property, or safety of AmbuCheck, our users, or others

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request access to your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Data Portability**: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our web services may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your login session and improve your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## Third-Party Services

Our application connects to backend services hosted on Render.com. These services process and store your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

## Children's Privacy

AmbuCheck is intended for use by medical professionals and ambulance service personnel. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
